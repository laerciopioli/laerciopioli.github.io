Exchange at
INESCTEC

In this research exchange I had the opportunity in working in of the biggest portuguese scientific institute for tecnology of systems & computing engineering, technology and science.

INESC TEC is a non-profit research association that operates privately and holds Public Interest status. Its primary focus is on the development of technology and scientific research, as well as technology transfer, advanced consultancy services, and training. Additionally, it assists in the pre-incubation phase of newly emerged technology-based companies. INESC TEC, an establishment situated at the intersection of academia, public administration, businesses, and society, functions by uniting these various entities. As such, it frequently employs the knowledge and outcomes produced by its research in technology transfer initiatives, with the goal of creating value and producing immediate social impact. INESC TEC's 13 R&D Centres are organized based on four thematic domains: Computer Science, Industrial and Systems Engineering, Networked Intelligent Systems, and Power and Energy. At INESC TEC, there are more than 700 researchers integrated into the institution. This is composed of roughly 350 individuals with PhDs, as well as staff researchers, Higher Education Institution researchers, grant recipients, and affiliated researchers.
